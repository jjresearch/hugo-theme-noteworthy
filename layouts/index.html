{{ end }} Why compositionality?

Natural phenomenon are complex. On the surface, the task of understanding such complex natural phemonenon seems impossible. The miracle is that the natural world is structured. One of the general properties of structure is that it tends to be compositional. Compositionality is what allows complex structures to be coherent and therefore learnable.

Why interdisciplinary?

No one scientific domain owns the concept of compositionality. Each domain has its own seemingly different utilization of compositionality.

Through the lens of interdisciplinary research, we can understand compositionality. When we see compositionality in natural language processing, vision, structural biology, material science and graphic design, we see a more complete (or less-dogmatic) picture. We can begin to really see compositionality.

Why geometry?

Consider the space of compositional structures. (How does one even parameterize such a space?!) My hunch (conjecture, intuition) is that this space itself has a structure, and that structure is expressed through geometry.

Geometry is where the technical meets the abstract.
